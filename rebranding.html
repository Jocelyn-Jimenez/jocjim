Re-Branding Project


Role: Designer Made: Spring 2017 Team: N/A

Project Description: We were asked to walk around Deep Ellum and find a store that caught our attention. The goal was to study the store, understand its vibe and who were the stakeholders. Then we had to re-design the store logo and some of its items. I chose to study a very cute clothing store that also had office supplies and diy terrariums. The following was my attempt at capturing my understanding of the store through design.
