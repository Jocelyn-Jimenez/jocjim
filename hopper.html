Hopper Project


Role: Designer Made: Fall 2018 for Design Nature course Team: N/A

Project Description: Designed a hopper that jumped at least 4 inches with a trigger. Hopper was designed to assist my persona, which was the “Yellowstone Vigilante”, a wolf who’s goal was to restore the balance of its habitat and river. The hopper was designed to camouflage with other dears who are ruining the river grounds.
