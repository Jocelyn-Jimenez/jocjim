Friendzone


Role: Researcher & Designer Made: Spring 2019 for Products and Marketing Course Team: Abigail Nordwall, Odalys Benitez, and Solomon Greenberg

Project Description: The following project was for our Products & Market final. As incoming First Years, we realized the lack of sexual harassment prevention tools/awareness on college campuses. We interviewed multiple students from the BOW Consortium (Babson, Olin, Wellesley) and came up with Friendzone. Friendzone is a rough prototype of a mobile application that allows college students to attend college parties and have fun while remaining safe. The app holds everyone accountable to each other, keeps track of well-being, and gives rewards for those who were safe and responsible.
