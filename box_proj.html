Self-Designed Box


Role: Designer Made: Spring 2020 for Introduction to Mechanical Prototyping Team: N/A

Project Description: My box is called “Walking Man”, a tribute to a statue in Deep Ellum that I love very dearly. Deep Ellum is a small city on the outskirts of Downtown Dallas, where my school was located, that exposed me to a lot of innovative artistic start-ups, murals and street artists. I fell in love with the culture of the area and the fact that people from diverse backgrounds came together in this area to creatively demonstrate their love for art to the world. I wanted to create a functional art-piece that would hold my art-supplies. My box has a total of 10 sides, 6 types of Joints (Butt Joint x4, Slot Joint x2, Finger Joint, T-Joint, Cross-Lap Joint, and a Hinge) and 5 fasteners (Screws, Nails, Wood Glue, String, and Tape).
