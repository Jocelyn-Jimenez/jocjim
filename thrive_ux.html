Thrive


Role: Member Made: Fall 2020 in UX Design Course Team: Riya Aggarwal, Audrey Lee, Rachel Navarrette, & Mason Grabowski

Project Description: Gardeners have a multitude of resources where they can search for help. However, there is the problem of too many resources in different locations with sometimes contradicting information. Another problem that needs to be addressed is the frustration with the gardening process: why their plants are sick or dying or have died. We want to improve the gardening experience and lower the barrier to entry to the gardening world.

This project is still in process.
