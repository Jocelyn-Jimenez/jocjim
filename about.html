I'm Jocelyn!

Concentration in User Experience and Interface Design



I am an undergraduate engineering student passionate about ethics, design and sustainability. My goal is to improve accessibility for marginalized communities through sustainable measures.


I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. I come from a very poor, majority minority neighborhood, where most of my classmates were raised in low-income households, whose parents were exposed to various hardships, like discrimination, work-related health issues, and deportation. I credit these experiences with the development of my passion to help others.


It has been difficult to become the engineer I want to be, but I am happy that despite all of the obstacles I have seen and faced, I have only become more passionate about helping to improve our neighborhoods.
