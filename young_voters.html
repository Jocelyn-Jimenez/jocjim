Rising Voters


Role: Researcher & Designer Made: Fall 2020 Team: Ivonne Munoz & Patrick Ogunbufunmi

Project Description: For the course Democracy in Action, we were asked to do an outreach project, in where we decided to learn more about how high school students are engaging in this new virtual environment and their current engagement with politics (voter registration, voting rights, political debates, etc.).

After interviewing some educators and surveying multiple high school students around the nation, we created an Instagram Feed (@rising.voters) that touches on important information voters should consider or know about voting. This was due to the fact that most students were currently engaged in social media and shared a concern of not being able to share their voice because they did not know how to or the benefits with doing so through voting.
