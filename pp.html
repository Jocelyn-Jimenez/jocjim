Citizen Science


Role: Kit Designer Made: Spring 2019 for Paper Panacea course Kit Team: Camille Girard, Mason Grabowski, and Katie Thai-Tang

Project Description: As a class, our goal was to create a citizen science project that would be very cheap and accessible to all. The kit would allow people to detect lead within their main water source for cheap. My group worked more specifically on making instructions clear and accessible. We all worked on creating symbols, but I helped make sure the final product was tied together.
