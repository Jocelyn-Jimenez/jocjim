Ethics and Context Research


Role: Researcher & Designer Made: Summer 2019 Team: Erhardt Graeff, Sam Daitzman, Austin Veseliza, and Melissa Anthony

Project Description: The purpose of the research group was to integrate Ethics and Context into Olin's culture and curriculum. During the span of 10 weeks, work was broken down into 7 different projects. We worked on integrating ethics in technical courses like: Software Design, Quantitative Engineering Analysis, and Machine Learning. The following are some of deliverables that were used to promote our research at Olin and encourage others to have these difficult conversations.
